Teacher Guide

Welcome to the Teacher Portal!

This section provides comprehensive guidance on using the portal features. Select a topic below to learn more.

Steps to Add a Student:

1. Navigate to the 'Add Student' section from the sidebar.

2. Fill in all required fields under Personal, Contact, Parental, Residence, and Financial Information.

3. Click the 'Add Student' button to save the data to the system.

Tip: Ensure the Reg No is unique and follows the format (e.g., AID/00119/025).

Steps to Upload Marks:

1. Go to the 'Upload Marks' section from the sidebar.

2. Select the class, subject, and term from the dropdowns.

3. Enter CAT (if applicable), Exam marks, and grades for each student in the table.

4. Click 'Save Marks' to submit.

Tip: Grades should be in A-F format with optional + or - (e.g., A+, B-).

Steps to Add an Announcement:

1. Navigate to the 'Announcements' section from the sidebar.

2. Choose 'General' or 'Individual' announcement type.

3. For individual announcements, select a student; then enter a title and message.

4. Click 'Add Announcement' to publish.

Tip: Use clear titles to make announcements easy to find.

Steps to Promote Students:

1. Go to the 'Students' section from the sidebar.

2. Use the filters to select the current class and stream (if applicable).

3. In the 'Bulk Student Promotion' section, select the class to promote to from the dropdown.

4. Click 'Promote Students' and confirm the action.

Note: Streams remain the same; only the class changes. This action affects all filtered students and cannot be undone.

What is CBC (Competency-Based Curriculum)?

The Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) is an educational framework adopted in Kenya that focuses on developing learners' competencies and skills rather than rote memorization. It emphasizes practical learning, critical thinking, and real-world application.

Key features include:

  • Learner-centered approach
  • Integration of values and life skills
  • Continuous assessment
  • Flexibility in learning pathways

CBC aims to produce self-reliant individuals who can contribute to society effectively.

Importance of the Students Portal

The Students Portal is a crucial tool for enhancing the learning experience and fostering communication between students, parents, and the school. It provides easy access to important information and resources.

Key benefits include:

  • Access to Academic Information: Students can view their marks, results, and progress reports anytime.
  • Announcements and Updates: Receive timely school announcements, events, and important notices.
  • Parental Involvement: Parents can monitor their child's performance and stay engaged in their education.
  • Resource Sharing: Access learning materials, assignments, and study resources.
  • Efficiency: Reduces paperwork and streamlines administrative processes.

Overall, the portal promotes transparency, accountability, and a collaborative learning environment.
